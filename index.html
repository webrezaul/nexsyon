Our Capabilities

Automotive Process

The Automotive SPICE (Software Process Improvement and Capability determination) is a process assessment model, commonly used in the Automotive Software industry, to evaluate the quality of a software by considering a set of indicators involved in its development process  

Autosar

Automotive Open System Architecture is a worldwide standardization initiative of leading automotive car Manufacturers (OEMs), Suppliers (Tier 1 & Tier 2) and other companies from electronics, semiconductor and software industry “Cooperate on standards, compete on implementation”  

Embedded Software

software running on a special purpose computer system designed to perform a dedicated function and it’s optimized to meet constraints: performance , timing , power , memory and cost. This software is typically specialized for particular hardware and sometimes referred to as Firmware  

Embedded Linux

The combination of Cross-compilation toolchain , Linux kernel , bootloader and root file system Adapted to usage in Embedded applications which allow us to design the system with high quality components at the foundation and to focus on the added value of the product  

Security

Security is the ability of a system to set up mechanisms to protect its resources which requires confidentiality, integrity, availability, authentication, authorisation, accountability and non-repudiation (can’t deny the fact of performing an action)  

Safety

Functional safety is the absence of unreasonable risk due to hazards caused by malfunctioning behaviour of Electronic systems in another words: the system functionality should FAIL in a SAFE way
